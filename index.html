The Founder

Born from a
kitchen, built
on passion.

"I never set out to start a business. I just wanted to bake something that would make people feel the way homemade food made me feel — held, loved, and at home."

BakeMyDay.gh was founded by a passionate baker whose love for banana bread turned from a weekend ritual into something far bigger — a brand that now reaches families, offices, and loved ones across Accra and beyond.

How It Started

Overripe bananas
and a family recipe.

It started simply — a handful of overripe bananas, a grandmother's handwritten recipe, and a Sunday afternoon with nothing but time. The result was so good that it couldn't stay in the kitchen.

Word spread quickly among friends and family. Then came the first proper order — a birthday gift for someone's mother. Then a wedding favour. Then a corporate hamper. Within months, BakeMyDay.gh had become something real: Accra's favourite artisan banana bread brand.

Every loaf still carries that same intention — to bring a moment of warmth to whoever receives it. Nothing has changed except the reach.

From birthdays to baby showers, from Sunday mornings to office celebrations — every occasion is made sweeter with a loaf that was made with intention. We've watched our breads spark smiles at weddings, comfort grieving families, and celebrate promotions. Joy, baked in.
